Sports & Adventure

"Jobs fill your pocket, adventures fill your soul" ~Jaime Lyn Beatty

Mitali Nopany has done skiing, ice skating, scuba diving and bridge swinging. She is interested in trying out sky diving and bungee.

She enjoying skiing in Switzerland and Kashmir in India. Every year she tries to take out time to go on a skiing adventure. She has done scuba diving in Andaman & Nicobar Island in India and in parts of Italy. She loves the experience of being surrounded by water and the world down there. She finds it to be the most peaceful place on Earth.

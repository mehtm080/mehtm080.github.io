Mitali Nopany was born on 10th October 1996, in Mumbai, India. She lives with her parents, elder sister and younger brother. She studied in BD Somani International School till grade 9 and then in 2011 moved to Baroda in Gujrat. Her interest in art started growing and she decided to make it her career.

Currently she is studying Communication Design in Parsons School for Design in New York City. She is interested in making a future in advertising. She is experiencing a thrilling, fast paced life, which is a massive transition from the eighteen years that she has spent in this small city. Being from a conservative Indian family, her future ventures are yet unclear. She will either become a homemaker just like her mother or will get to live and breathe her passion of being a designer. Parsons is giving her the opportunity to be exposed to fervent and determined individuals, who, just like her are set out to leave their mark on the world.
